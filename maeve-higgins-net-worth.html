A Star in the Making

Her foray into comedy was marked by early appearances on The Ray D’Arcy Show on Today FM, setting the stage for her ascension in the Irish comedy circuit.

Her participation in Naked Camera alongside P.J. Gallagher showcased her knack for hidden camera antics and situational comedy, leading her to create and star in her own show, Fancy Vittles.
