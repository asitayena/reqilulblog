Marion Cotillard’s Breakthrough Roles and Filmography

Marion first captured international attention with her role in La Vie en Rose, where she portrayed the iconic French singer Édith Piaf. This performance not only won her the Academy Award for Best Actress but also catapulted her into Hollywood stardom.

Following this, Marion starred in a range of influential films including Inception and Midnight in Paris, balancing her work between Hollywood blockbusters and profound French cinema projects. Each role not only added to her fame but also significantly increased her earning power.
