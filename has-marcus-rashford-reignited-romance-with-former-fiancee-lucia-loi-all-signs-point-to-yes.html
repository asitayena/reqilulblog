Has Marcus Rashford reignited romance with former fiance Lucia Loi? All signs point to yes

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  230 words  |  Billy Koelling

Marcus Rashford, the 26-year-old star striker for Manchester United, has been sparking reconciliation rumors with his former fiancée Lucia Loi after the pair was spotted on a date night last week.

Their joint appearance, the first since their break-up, has ignited speculation about a possible reunion.

According to The Sun, Marcus reserved the cellar of a French wine bar, which can accommodate up to 18 people, for his evening with Lucia.

The lavish evening included bottles of champagne reportedly priced at £950.

A source revealed, ‘They were there for hours laughing and joking. It’s a very nice place and he booked the whole of it for just the two of them. He couldn’t stop smiling.’

Since their apparent reunion, Lucia has been notably without the engagement ring Marcus used to propose to her.

In June, it was reported that the couple had ended their engagement two months prior, with Marcus seen enjoying his single life in Miami.

During his time in Miami, it was claimed that he enjoyed exclusive accommodations and was seen socializing with women.

According to a source, Marcus and Lucia called off their engagement as their relationship had run its course, but they remain close friends with mutual respect for each other.

A source has stated, ‘Marcus is laser-focused on the next phase of his football career and is putting all of his focus into that.’
