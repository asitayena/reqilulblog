You Me At Six Announce 'Take Off Your Colours' Anniversary Tour

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  259 words  |  Kelle Repass

You Me At Six have sent fans intro frenzy after finally announcing their ‘Take Off Your Colours’ anniversary tour.

After actively teasing the possibility of a 10 year anniversary tour off an on for years, the You Me At Six boys confirmed the three date tour via Twitter on July 23rd.

So excited to finally announce our Take Off Your Colours 10 Year Anniversary tour! We're hitting up Manchester, Glasgow and London in Nov/ Dec. Pre-sale begins 9AM BST on Wednesday 25th, be ready! https://t.co/yAm8rugCVd pic.twitter.com/LQUn9cl5dW

— You Me At Six (@youmeatsix) July 23, 2018

The British rock band will hit up London, Manchester and Glasgow across November and December, performing their debut album in full for the first time since its release back in 2008. The three-date tour will coincide with their already scheduled shows in support of their latest album ‘VI’.

Pre sale begins on Wendesday 25th July at 9am (BST) with general sale beginning on Friday 27th July. Below are all of You Me At Six’s UK tour shows with those playing Take Off Your Colours highlighted.

Thurs 22 Nov – Birmingham, O2 Academy
Fri 23 Nov – Manchester, Victoria Warehouse
Sat 24 Nov – Manchester, Victoria Warehouse (performing TOYC)
Tues 27 Nov – Glasgow, Barrowland
Wed 28 Nov – Glasgow, Barrowland (performing TOYC)
Fri 30 Nov – London, O2 Academy Brixton
Mon 01 Dec – Cardiff University Great Hall
Sun 02 Dec – London, O2 Academy Brixton (performing TOYC)
