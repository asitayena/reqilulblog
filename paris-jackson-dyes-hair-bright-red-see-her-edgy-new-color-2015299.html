Paris Jackson Dyes Hair Bright Red: See Her Edgy New Color

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  136 words  |  Tandra Barner

Hair alert! Paris Jackson debuted a new fiery crimson color while out for sushi in Calabasas, Calif. on Sunday, Sept. 27.

The 17-year-old daughter of the late Michael Jackson rocked the edgy red ombre style, jet black at the roots, with a contrasting olive green tank, ripped jeans, and casual black Chucks.

She accessorized with a statement pebble necklace and multiple leather bracelets. Jackson kept the focus on her green eyes with her signature wing-tipped cat-eye makeup. 

In June, Jackson had subtly dyed her ends the bold color, but now her hair is brighter. She posted a photo the day before to Instagram, flaunting her new style and captioning the pic, "Thank you guys so much for 100K followers!!! #loveyouall."
