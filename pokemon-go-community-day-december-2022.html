Pokemon Go Community Day December 2022

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  418 words  |  Tandra Barner

Trainers, it’s time for the last Community Day event for 2022 and just like all previous Community Days in December, you will have a chance to catch all previous Community Day Pokemon from 2021 and 2022.

Community Day is happening once per month, where players have the chance to catch a Pokemon that is often chosen by Niantic but only for a few hours. During those special hours, players can enjoy bonuses and make their Pokemon (the chosen one) learn a new move.
