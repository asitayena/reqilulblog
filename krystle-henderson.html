Krystle Henderson Bio | Wiki

Krystle Henderson is an American multimedia journalist, meteorologist, and anchor. She currently serves as a meteorologist at 12 News in Phoenix, Arizona. Krystle is part of the Today in AZ team, leading weather coverage, alongside Ryan Cody and Rachel McNeill.

She has received three 2019 Rocky Mountain Emmy Awards, Best Weather Anchor, Best Sports News Single Story, and Best Specialty Assignment Report. Krystle was also awarded Emmys for Talent-anchor-weather in 2017 and Talent-anchor-traffic in 2016. She served at KTVL News 10, the CBS affiliate.
