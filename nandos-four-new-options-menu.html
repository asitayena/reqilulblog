Nando's adds four new options to their autumn menu - and it looks delicious!

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  327 words  |  Billy Koelling

Nando's is the second best fast food chain in the UK

    Exciting news for Nando's fans! The popular South African restaurant has updated its autumn menu with four new fiery additions. From this week, Nando's lovers can indulge in the Fino Pitta, the Sweet Potato & Butternut Burger or the Supergreen Burger – as well as adding the Vusa Perinaise to their dish. The Fino Pitta gets an extra special Fino upgrade, as a toasted pitta is introduced to two flame-grilled PERi-PERi chicken thighs, topped with grilled halloumi cheese, caramelised red onion relish, cos lettuce and wild garlic aioli mayonnaise.

    The Fino Pitta gets an extra special Fino upgrade

    The Sweet Potato & Butternut Burger is packed full of the good stuff like sweet potato, butternut, red pepper, onion and edamame beans, or the Supergreen Burger, combining everything from broccoli to edamame beans to kale, veggie fans are in for a treat. Available as a burger, pitta or wrap, these winter-blues banishers are sure to be a hit! And finally, to really fire fans up through the chilly season, Vusa Perinaise is arriving to the Nando's menu for a limited time only. Think the fieriness of PERi Vusa meets the creaminess of PERinaise. This tasty treat is not for the faint-hearted.

    There are two new veggie options

    The new additions come shortly after the beloved restaurant came the second best fast food chain in the UK. The Afro-Portugese inspired eatery, which came behind Five Guys, has won a legion of fans including Prince Harry and Kanye West. So make sure you head down to your nearest Nando's to try the new menu items from Tuesday 17th October.
