Who Is Jeff Goldblum?

Jeffrey Lynn Goldblum, born October 22, 1952, in West Homestead, Pennsylvania, is an American actor and musician. His father, Leonard, was a physician and US Army major during the second world war. At the same time, his mother, Shirley, was a radio broadcaster who later ran a kitchen equipment and appliances sales firm. 

At 17, Jeffery moved to New York City to become an actor. He worked on the stage and studied acting at the renowned Neighborhood Playhouse under the guidance of acting coach Sanford Meisner. 

He made his Broadway debut in the musical “Two Gentlemen of Verona” in 1971 and his film debut as a home-invading thug in the 1974 film “Death Wish.” Goldblum also appeared in commercials for prominent brands like Toyota and lent his voice to numerous Apple commercials for their iMac and iBook during the 1990s.

The actor has been in some Hollywood blockbusters such as “Law & Order: Criminal Intent,” “Jurassic Park,” and “Thor,” to name a few. In 2014, Goldblum reignited his musician side and made a jazz band called “The Mildred Snitzer Orchestra.” 

The band gained some fame and did a weekly show at Carlyle Hotel. Billboard reported on May 29, 2018, that Decca Records asked Goldblum’s band to record a studio album for a later-year release, and the band subsequently released two more jazz albums over the years. 

Goldblum was the subject of the 2021 biography “Because He’s Jeff Goldblum,” written by Washington Post writer Travis M. Andrews. Goldblum has bagged some industry’s most coveted awards and nominations and still acts today. His estimated net worth is around $40 million.

Is Jeff Goldblum Gay?

No, Jeff Goldblum is not gay. He has been in several relationships with women, some of which have led to marriage. Despite the many rumors, no credible evidence suggests or proves he is gay.

Rumors regarding his sexuality made the rounds when a video of him circulated on the internet in September 2019. At a New Orleans LGBTQ+ summer party, observers witnessed the actor dancing to Normani’s “Motivation”

He wore a leopard print shirt and tiger print trousers, and some fans likened his outfit to what a gay man would wear. Goldblum has not said much in response to these rumors, but his three previous marriages to women are enough to speak for him.

How Many Times Has Jeff Goldblum Been Married?

The “Death Wish” star has entered into three marriages, with two of them being co-stars from some of his movies. Let’s take a look at his marital history.

Patricia Gaul

Goldblum met fellow actor Patricia Gaul, with whom he worked on the set of the popular TV show “Silverado.” They entered matrimony in 1980 and remained together until their divorce in 1985. They did not have any kids together. 

Geena Davis

Two years after his first divorce, Goldblum married co-star Geena Davis in 1987. The two were featured in several movies together, including “Transylvania 6-5000” (1985), “The Fly” (1986), and “Earth Girls Are Easy” (1988).

In October 1990, Davis initiated the divorce proceedings, which concluded in 1991.

Emilie Livingston

In 2014, Emilie Livingston, a Canadian athlete, and former Olympian, became the spouse of the “California Split” star.

Fans expressed discontent with their union due to the age difference of about thirty years between Goldblum and Emilie. They have two sons, Charlie and River.

Who Is Jeff Goldblum Currently Married To?

Emilie Livingston was born on January 4, 1983. People often label her as ‘multi-talented’ due to her roles as a retired athlete, former Olympic gymnast, and actress specializing in body doubles.
