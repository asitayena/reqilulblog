Suzy Amis Cameron is an American environmental advocate, a former actress, and a former model.

Suzy Amis Cameron age

Suzy Amis Cameron was born on January 5, 1962 in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, United States.

Suzy Amis Cameron height

Suzy Amis Cameron is quite tall and has a height of 1.73m.

Suzy Amis Cameron young

Born in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, Suzy Amis Cameron worked as a Ford model before she began acting in the 1980s.

Suzy Amis Cameron made her feature-film debut in the 1985 comedy film Fandango and had roles in Rocket Gibraltar (1988), Where the Heart Is (1990), and Rich in Love (1993).

In 1993, she appeared as Josephine “Jo” Monaghan in The Ballad of Little Jo.

She later appeared in Blown Away (1994), The Usual Suspects (1995), and the blockbuster Titanic (1997), in which she played Lizzy Calvert, the granddaughter of Rose Dawson Calvert (Gloria Stuart).

Suzy Amis Cameron school

In 2006, Suzy Amis Cameron founded MUSE School CA, with her sister Rebecca Amis, to prepare young people to live consciously with themselves, one another and the planet.
