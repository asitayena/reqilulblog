Timea Babos is a Hungarian professional tennis player who is best known for being a former world No. 1 in doubles and for winning several tournaments such as Australian Open, French Open, and WTA Tour. She has also participated in a variety of other tournaments such as Wimbledon Championships, ITF Junior Circuit, Osaka Mayor’s Cup, Cincinnati Open, Brasil Tennis Cup, St. Petersburg Trophy, Sydney International, Gippsland Trophy, Dubai Tennis Championship, and many more. She has been sponsored by brands like Fila, Adidas, Samsung, and K-Swiss.
