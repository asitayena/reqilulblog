Who is Chris McNally Girlfriend Julie Gonzalo?

Julie Gonzalo is an Argentinian-American actress who rose to fame as Pamela Rebecca Barnes from the TV romantic drama series "Dallas." Besides Dallas, she is also known as Becky Adams in the comedy-drama film "Waffle Street" (2015).

Born on 9th September 1981 as Julieta Susana Gonzalo in Lanus, Buenos Aires, Argentina, she spent her entire childhood in Miami, Florida, USA. Before she made her acting debut, she was a print model, and as her modeling career progressed, she moved to Los Angeles as she felt that she would have more chances of showcasing her acting skills. 

In 2012, Julie reached stardom with the role of Pamela Rebecca Barnes in the TV romantic-drama series "Dallas," in which she featured in all 40 episodes, which launched her to prominence. Following the end of the series in 2014, Julie continued her career rather successfully, first with the lead role in the film "Waffle Street" (2015).
