Relationship History Of YouTuber Emma Chamberlain

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  542 words  |  Patria Henriques

Emma Chamberlain is a popular YouTuber renowned for her relatable material. She recently appeared as a guest on the Call Her Daddy podcast, where she discussed her channel, relationships, and other topics. Chamberlain typically keeps her love life quiet, but on this particular occasion, she spoke out about her previous romances without identifying anybody. Here are some of her comments regarding her ex-boyfriends:

“I discovered that in previous relationships, I didn’t feel as valued by the males I dated. Because I feel like they sort of, one specific connection in particular, but it was just like, I just felt like, you know, he was really judgmental of the fact that I wasn’t a particularly feminine lady.” “I felt as if I wasn’t enough, and that the places where I was feminine weren’t enough, therefore I felt as if there was an attempt to make me more feminine.”

Fans assume she was referring to her ex, Ethan Dolan. In 2018-19, the two were thought to be dating while simultaneously working on material for their group “Sister Squad,” which included Emma Chamberlain, James Charles, and the Dolan Twins. Chamberlain is also said to be dating Tucker Pillsbury, popularly known as Role Model. He is a singer, composer, and former rapper from the United States. Emma Chamberlain’s dating history may be seen on her YouTube channel.

A look into Emma Chamberlain’s relationships.

1) Ethan Dolan

Rumors of a relationship between Dolan and Chamberlain began in early 2018 when the two were regularly seen together and working on stuff. They were also members of the Sister Squad and collaborated on many videos. When Dolan revealed to dating someone in late 2018, people assumed it was Chamberlain based on their chemistry. Apparently, the two split up in 2019 and ceased producing video together.

2) Aaron Hull

Hull and Chamberlain’s relationship first came to light in late 2019, while the two were working together on projects. Chamberlain’s video “Learning How to Skateboard to Impress a Guy” fanned the suspicions even more. Hull is known to be a skateboarding lover. They did, however, split up sometime in January 2020. Hull addressed their relationship in a video he made in September of that year, claiming that the two just didn’t function well together, leading to their split. Among the things he says in the video are:

“Distance and everything else, the calculation was simply too difficult.” From my perspective, I believe.” “Emma was the best girlfriend I could have hoped for; I couldn’t have asked for anything more from her.” She was really encouraging to me.”

3)Tucker Pillsbury

Pillsbury and Chamberlain are said to have been dating since August 2020, since they have been sighted together many times. Chamberlain said on the podcast that she is in a relationship, which supporters assume is with Pillsbury. During the podcast, she said that her current relationship is “amazing” and “supporting,” and that she has no “complaints” with her spouse.


On being questioned about her YouTube journey, Emma Chamberlain explained that she has tried to shape her content according to her growth, and has not let the platform stop her from evolving and changing. She said that she may take a vacation from her channel, which has over 11 million subscribers, but she would not leave YouTube.
