Juawana Williams- Professional Life, Career

Juawana Williams started her work journey by being a vice president of Tampa’s Pasco Economic Development Council. She then worked as an advertising specialist for the US Small Business Administration.

For only five months she was the Associate Director at the company named Year Up. Her other experiences are Business Development Manager at Tampa Hillsborough Economic Development Corporation (2017 – Dec 2018), Manager, Regional Economic Development at Greater Houston Partnership (Apr 2012 – Jul 2014), and Project Manager at Charlotte Regional Partnership (Jul 2006 – Mar 2012).

In 2019, she joined the real estate industry with Allure Realty, LLC. She was appointed as a realtor and is still working there. The company was founded by Sharelle Rosado in 2019 and has eight skilled plus talented employees.

The other members are Rena Frazier, Tennille Moore, Colony Reeves, Anne-Sophie Petit-Frere, Alexis Williams, and Karla Giorgio.
